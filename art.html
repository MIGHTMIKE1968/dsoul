Art from the Streets
of Puerto Plata



Previous Next



By Paola Garcia | April 5, 2019




Street art in my hometown of Puerto Plata, DR is a large part of this community’s culture. There are so many amazing pieces of street art to be found all throughout my city. My town and its talented artists are starting to gain recognition outside of the Dominican Republic for their outstanding works. It is my pleasure to share some of this beautiful and diverse artwork with you.


Until next time. Besos!
