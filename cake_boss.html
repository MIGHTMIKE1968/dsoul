PUERTO PLATA WEATHER



By Paola Garcia | March 18, 2019




Like most people around the world, Dominicans enjoy celebrating special occasions with family and friends. Even more so, we love to celebrate them with cakes. Now we're not just talking about any type of cakes. We are talking about some of the biggest, most beautiful, artistically creative, themed cakes you will ever lay eyes on! Whether the special occasion is an anniversary, birthday, baby shower, graduation, wedding or some other important life event, it's virtually mandatory to get one of these cakes if you are Dominican.


The makers of these masterpieces, the Dominican Cake Bosses, place their heart, soul and immense baking skills into creating these culinary works of art that taste every bit as good as they look. Driven by a competitive passion to create while meeting the fervent demands of their customers high expectations, these bosses are constantly outdoing themselves with one elaborate and delicious cake right after the other. For them, it's more than just baking wonderful cakes. It's a labor of love.


It's my pleasure to share with you some of the eye-catching work of a couple of the Dominican Cake Bosses. Special thanks to the bosses over at Puerto Plata's very own Caliche Reposteria Bakery and Carmen's Dominican Cakes of Amityville, New York.
